depeche MODE

music that saved my life

I cannot believe that just a year ago all I knew about them was a few of their smash hits - Enjoy the Silence, Personal Jesus as well as Freelove (Flood remix) which I remembered because of the bizarre video and Precious which has stuck in my mind as it was often played on the radio in the days of my youth.

I'm waiting for 9th February to see them play live for the second time in my life.
